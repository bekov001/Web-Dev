Travel to the Wonders of Almaty

The Almaty region of Kazakhstan is a land of extremes. Within just a few hours' drive, you can travel from scorching red deserts to alpine lakes surrounded by snow-capped peaks.

The Valley of Castles

Charyn Canyon is often called the little brother of the Grand Canyon. Over millions of years, wind and water have carved the red sedimentary rock into shapes that look like ancient towers and fortresses.

The Sunken Forest

High in the Tian Shan mountains lies Kaindy Lake. Following an earthquake in 1911, a landslide created a natural dam, flooding a forest of spruce trees. Today, their trunks rise out of the turquoise water like the masts of ghost ships.

Must-Do Activities:

  • Hike the 2km trail through Charyn Canyon to the river.
  • Take a horseback ride through the Kolsai Lakes National Park.
  • Enjoy traditional Kazakh tea and baursak in a mountain yurt.

Best Times to Visit:

  1. May to June: For blooming wildflowers and green valleys.
  2. September to October: For golden autumn leaves and perfect hiking weather.
